Our Story

Built for Sacred Work That Deserves Excellence

Muslim organisations are doing deeply meaningful work — educating our children, serving communities, and delivering vital services. But too often, their growth is limited not by vision, but by capacity.

We saw organisations relying on freelancers with no ownership, outdated systems that slowed everything down, and marketing that failed to connect with the very communities it was meant to serve.

Shoaib Projects was created to change that.

We built a dedicated, trained team focused on Muslim organisations — a team that understands Islamic values, community dynamics, and modern growth strategies. Not as external vendors, but as committed partners who carry the mission alongside you.

Do you only work with Muslim organisations?

Our primary focus is on Muslim-led and values-aligned organisations, while we also collaborate with others who uphold the same high ethical standards and principles.

Do you work with small organisations?

Yes. We regularly support grassroots and volunteer-led initiatives, providing guidance, resources, and hands-on assistance to help communities achieve meaningful impact.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Long-term partnership is central to how we work. We stay by your side, building trust, aligning goals, and supporting your organisation’s growth over time.

How do we get started?
